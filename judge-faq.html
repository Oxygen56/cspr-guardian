Judge Questions

Is this more than a UI?

Yes. The agent discovers paid tools, pays, consumes four signals, rejects replay, records revenue, decides, and exports proof.

Is Casper meaningful?

Yes. Casper is the durable receipt and settlement-anchor layer, with one final receipt and four x402 settlement anchors.

Is this mainnet finance?

No. It is a testnet prototype that demonstrates payment, evidence, and audit mechanics without production custody claims.

Can judges verify without screenshots?

Yes. The proof room publishes JSON, Markdown, hashes, verifier output, audit output, and public CSPR.live links.
