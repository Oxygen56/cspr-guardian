# Winning Strategy

## Project Positioning

Buildathon judges are likely to reward projects that make the Casper-specific
agent economy tangible. CSPR Guardian should be pitched as infrastructure for
autonomous treasuries:

> Agents can buy risk intelligence from paid MCP services, make constrained
> allocation decisions, and leave a verifiable Casper audit trail.

This avoids the crowded "portfolio chatbot" category and shows a repeatable
business model for agent-to-agent payments.

## Prize Narrative

The highest-prize version demonstrates these things in one tight flow:

1. A paid MCP tool exists.
2. The agent receives 402 payment requirements and signs payment proofs.
3. Each x402 paid tool has a signed Casper transfer preflight path for real
   settlement.
4. Paid tools return RWA risk, KYB/sanctions, liquidity-depth, and covenant-monitoring signals.
5. The final decision is exported as an evidence bundle and anchored to Casper
   as an audit receipt.
6. The BUIDL page has concrete public repo, hosted demo, video, and CSPR.live
   explorer links.

## Competitive Benchmark

Publicly visible Casper Agentic Buildathon signals include spend guard,
multi-agent economy, governance sentinel, and RWA escrow cockpit projects. CSPR
Guardian should be positioned above generic agent demos by emphasizing one
reviewable loop: paid MCP discovery, x402-style Casper authorization, four RWA
intelligence purchases, replay protection, provider revenue, policy-bounded
allocation, independent verification, and a real Casper receipt.

## What Still Raises The Ceiling

These are optional next improvements. They are not remaining submission gates:

1. Broadcast a real Casper settlement transaction for at least one x402 tool
   payment, not only the final receipt.
2. Add a hosted provider dashboard with paid tool revenue across users.
3. Add a short architecture diagram showing agent, paid tools, verifier, and
   Casper receipt in one screen.

## Judging Hooks

- "Agent-to-agent commerce on Casper" is stronger than "AI chatbot for DeFi."
- RWA risk review gives a compliance/audit reason for Casper receipts.
- x402 unlocks micro-paid tools; Casper creates durable payment and decision
  provenance.
- A capped allocation policy makes the agent feel safe enough for real treasury
  workflows.
- A downloadable evidence bundle gives judges something concrete to inspect:
  x402 proofs, report hashes, the decision hash, the receipt hash, and the
  evidence hash.

## Submission Tagline

CSPR Guardian turns Casper into the payment and audit layer for autonomous RWA
treasury agents.
